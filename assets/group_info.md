Group description:

Data Science Club gathers all Data Science, ML, and AI professionals, entrepreneurs, students, enthusiasts, and popularizes in Serbia and Belgrade.
The range of topics is everything from the presentation of the latest technologies, scientific insights, solutions, and discussions about immediate business applications, to social, political, and cultural consequences of the application of ML and AI, problems of legal regulation, market conditions and popularization.
All events in our organization are free, and will remain free. All discussions are conducted in an open and accepting spirit. Join us!
